About Me

This is what I do.

HI, I’M LISA. I am the founder and lead designer for Fleur de Lisa Floral Co., a florist company that currently serve people in Utah and California. I have worked with flowers since 2014. I have been designing arrangements for many types of events and continue to learn and grow as my business does.

I love the creativity of the wedding industry, and strive to make each event that I do, a work of art. Flowers are amazing to me, and getting to work with them so often is a dream come true.

I have been married to my husband for the past 25 years, and we have five wonderful children and ten grandchildren. They are my everything, and I love being with them. When I’m not playing with flowers I love visiting my grand babies and traveling around in my van! #vanlife

Thank you for taking an interest in what I do, feel free to contact me if you are interested in hiring me for your special day or event!

- Lisa
